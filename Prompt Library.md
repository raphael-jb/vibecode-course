# Prompt Library

## Series: The Modern Leader's Weight
**Objective:** A series of 3 hyperreal images showing a consistent character navigating high-pressure leadership challenges in a modern office.
**Tool:** Gemini / Visual Direction Gem

### Character Anchor (The "Anchor" for all prompts)
**Character:** A 42-year-old German leadership figure. They have a "New Work" aesthetic: intelligent eyes with a hint of fatigue, short textured dark-blonde hair with subtle silver at the temples. 
**Style:** They wear a high-quality polo-knit in **Forest Green (#0E2616)** or **Navy**, reflecting the brand's grounding palette. No tie, no blazer. Distinct, matte-translucent olive-grey eyewear.
**Hero Rule:** The character must look **directly into the camera lens** with an emotionally legible expression.
**Environment Rule:** Environments should feature **Instrument Serif** typographic accents on walls or glass (if text is visible) and follow the **#FCF8F1 (Warm Cream)** light profile.

---

### Phase 1: The Sandwich Position (Company Level)
**Objective:** Illustrate the tension of being the bridge between levels.
**Strategy to avoid Stock-look:** Use heavy bokeh (blur) and "environmental layering." No visible spreadsheets or generic office signs.

**Prompt:**
Generate a hyperreal, editorial-style image of our hero character (42-year-old German leader, navy merino polo-knit, matte-translucent eyewear). 

**The Scene:** 
The character is in a modern, warm office corridor. In the deep background, behind a glass pane, we see the *blurred shapes* and *silhouettes* of a high-pressure board meeting—no faces, just the atmosphere of demand. In the extreme foreground, the *out-of-focus shoulder* of a team member is visible, creating a sense of being "closed in."

**The Action & Emotion:** 
The character stands perfectly still in the center, looking **directly into the camera**. Their expression is one of "calm endurance" amidst the surrounding movement. They are the only sharp, still element in a world of blurred pressure.

**Visual DNA:** 
Golden-hour sunlight hitting the glass partitions. Warm, natural wood textures. **Shallow depth of field (bokeh)** is critical to keep the focus on the emotion. 16:9 aspect ratio.

---

### Phase 2: The Silent Gap (Team Level)
**Objective:** The feeling of subtle isolation despite being present.
**Strategy to avoid Stock-look:** Avoid "team smiles." The team should be a background texture, not a group portrait.

**Prompt:**
Generate a hyperreal, editorial-style image of our hero character (42-year-old German leader, navy merino polo-knit, matte-translucent eyewear).

**The Scene:** 
A warm, design-conscious office lounge area at dusk. In the background, a small group of colleagues is huddled together in a warm, blurred circle of light, laughing and talking—they feel like a distant island of ease.

**The Action & Emotion:** 
The character is in the foreground, holding a dark ceramic cup. They are looking **directly into the camera** with a look of "perceptive loneliness." They are physically close but emotionally on the other side of a gap. 

**Visual DNA:** 
Soft, indirect warm light. Deep shadows. Lived-in atmosphere (plants, textiles). The hero is the only sharp point of focus. 16:9 aspect ratio.

---

### Phase 3: The Individual Burden (Individual Level)
**Objective:** The solitary moment where everything lands on one person.
**Strategy to avoid Stock-look:** Keep it minimalist, like the first successful draft.

**Prompt:**
Generate a hyperreal, editorial-style image of our hero character (42-year-old German leader, navy merino polo-knit, matte-translucent eyewear).

**The Scene:** 
The exact same minimalist office from the first draft. The sun is lower now, casting long, dramatic but soft shadows across the wooden desk. No technology is glowing—the laptop is closed. 

**The Action & Emotion:** 
The character is sitting on the edge of the desk, hands folded. They are looking **directly into the camera** with an expression of "quiet truth." The weight is visible in the eyes, but the posture remains professional.

**Visual DNA:** 
High-contrast golden light. Rich navy wool texture. A sense of "the day is over, the weight remains." 16:9 aspect ratio.
